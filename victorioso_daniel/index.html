My Goals

Career Goals

As a second-year IT student, my short-term goal is to gain hands-on experience through internships, projects, and collaborations that allow me to apply what I’ve learned in real-world scenarios. In the long term, I aspire to build a successful career as a Software Developer or Cybersecurity Specialist, contributing to innovative solutions that solve complex problems and make a positive impact.

Skill Development Goals

I’m focused on mastering foundational skills in programming languages like Python/Java, web development, and database management while also exploring emerging technologies like blockchain. Additionally, I want to improve my soft skills, such as communication and teamwork, to become a well-rounded IT professional.

Personal Goals

Balancing academics and personal growth is important to me. I aim to stay curious, explore new hobbies, and maintain a healthy lifestyle while pursuing my passion for technology. I also hope to inspire my peers by sharing knowledge and collaborating on projects that challenge us to grow together.

Entrepreneurial Goals

While I’m still early in my journey, I dream of one day creating my own app, or software solution that addresses a real-world problem. I want to use my technical skills to build something meaningful and impactful, whether it’s a tool, service, or platform that benefits others.

Learning and Growth Goals

As a student, I’m committed to continuous learning. My goal is to complete free certifications like HackerRank skill badges (e.g., Python, Java, or Problem Solving) and LeetCode skill badges to strengthen my coding and problem-solving abilities. Additionally, I plan to pursue Google’s free certifications, such as the Google Cloud Fundamentals, to gain foundational knowledge in cloud computing. Beyond certifications, I also plan to attend workshops, hackathons, and tech events to expand my network and stay updated on industry trends.
